# **App Name**: CardConnect Dubai

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display a catalog of smart business cards with various designs, materials, and features.
- Card Customization: Allow users to customize their smart business cards with logos, contact information, and preferred sharing methods (NFC, QR code, etc.).
- Order Placement: Enable users to place orders for customized cards, specifying quantity, shipping address in Dubai, and payment details.
- Shipping and Tracking: Provide order status updates and tracking information for deliveries within Dubai.
- Customer Accounts: Allow customers to manage their profiles, addresses, order history and preferred payment methods.
- AI-Powered Design Assistant: Offers AI-driven suggestions for card design layouts, font choices, and content based on user's profession and industry, serving as a helpful tool in creating an effective business card.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant blue (#29ABE2) to evoke trust and innovation, mirroring Dubai's tech-forward approach.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5), providing a clean, professional backdrop for product displays.
- Accent color: A warm yellow (#FFC107) to highlight key actions and promotions.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, for clear, readable product descriptions and customer information.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a sans-serif with a tech-modern feel for titles.
- Use sleek, modern icons to represent card features, customization options, and delivery services.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance the user experience during customization and checkout.
